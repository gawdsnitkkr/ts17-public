Social Initiatives

by

Techspardha may be just technical fest for some but we as a team see it as a fest which relates with the all around upliftment of the society.Being an elite technical institution in the state it is our sole responsibility to mend the stereotypes in the society.
Being technical grads does not mean an end to our zeal, since inception we have strived for a more substantial cause. India has a great gender demography at present with 60% of youth population. What we need is to have a society free of prejudices, social stigmas and other stereotypes towards women which has engulfed the present generation ultimately leading to its annihilation. ‘She Save her’ and ‘Make A Wish’ are some of the social campaigns that targets amelioration of the women’s perception and children condition in India particularly Haryana which is socially lagging in women empowerment.

She - Save her

It’s a social campaign in which the students of our college would visit the surrounding villages of our campus and among the rural areas of Haryana and promote women welfare and girl welfare schemes of the government and to make citizens aware of the female conditions in the society and to eradicate the gender discrimination and female foeticide in the society.

Make a wish

It’s a philanthropist initiative in which the underpriviledged children in the proximity of our campus are told to make a wish, which are fulfilled by the opulent members of our college if possible. It make them feel a sense of belonging with the campus.

MISSION

To have a deep insight into the root cause of the social problems faced by the minority masses in the patriarch society and pledge to have a society based on gender equality and empathy by modulating the mindset of the present generation and formulating that of the future ones.

VISION

To have an egalitarian society free of social stigmas by taking up social initiatives on a monthly basis throughout Haryana, and to promote awareness among the people of the child rights and women empowerment.
